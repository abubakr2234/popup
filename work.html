SIGNATORY OF UNPRI

The Principle for Responsible Investment reflects the increase
relevance of enviromental,social and corporate governance issues to
investment practices. South Suez will only invest with fund managers that
have ESG policie that are in line with the key standards set by the UNPRI.

In signing the Principle, we as investors publicly commit to adopt and
implemented them, where consistent with our fiduciary responsible.
We also evaluate the effectivness and improve the content of the
Principle over time,Aiming to improve our ability to meet commitments
to beneficiaries as well as better align our investment activities with the
broader intrests of society.

IFC SUSTAINABILITY FRAMEWORK

South Suez requires fund managers to comply at a minimum with the legal
regulatory requirements in the jurisdictions in which they operate and to
work towards relevant interational best practice standards.This includes
the IFC'S Sustainability Framework which has become a benchmark for
enviromental and social risk management amougst our fund managers.

We use this framework to classify our portfolio companies into different
ESG risk categories:Category A being business activities with potential
significant adverse enviromental or social risk and/or impacts that are
diverse,irreversible, or unprecedented. This category is closely
monitored through their ESG reports
